### How do I start a free 7-day Trial?

The 7-day free Trial starts automatically after you register on Gologin!

To register in the app, just download the app at [https://gologin.com/download/](https://gologin.com/download/) and install it on your device. You will be presented with the registration window at the first launch.

### Are Browser Profiles any different on a Trial plan other than on a Paid one? Are they any worse?

No, they are not any worse or any different. You are getting the same quality on a Trial plan to test the features.

### How do I buy a subscription plan?

To purchase a subscription plan, go to the **main menu at the top-left \> Settings & Members \> Billing Plans \> Change plan**

### When does my subscription end? When is the next subscription charge?

To get the date, go to **main menu at the top-left \>** **Settings & Members \> Billing plans \> Payment history** section. The **Expires on** date would be the date when the plan ends and the date of the next payment.\
You won't see the exact timestamp of the subscription ending, however, you can approximate it based on the time of your most recent payment. Your next subscription charge will happen within an hour before or after your subscription ends.

### How do I get my invoice or receipt?

**Main menu at the top-left \>** **Settings & Members \> Billing plans \> Payment history \> Download receipt**\
For more details, please look into [<u>this article</u>](/account-migration-support/account-and-billing/invoices).

### What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit and debit cards, PayPal, Crypto, WeChat, Pix and bank account payments.\
For more details, look into the [payment methods](/general/account-and-billing/payment-methods) article.

### How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to **main menu at the top-left \> Settings & Members \> Billing Plans \> Cancel Subscription**. Your profiles remain accessible until the current billing period ends.

### Does canceling automatically refund my payment?

No. Cancellation stops future charges but does not trigger an automatic refund. To request a refund, contact support via the in-app chat. Gologin offers a **7-day money-back guarantee** for new subscriptions.

### How long does a refund take?

Typically **5 business days**. In rare cases, it may take up to 25 business days depending on your payment provider.

### How do I downgrade my plan?

Downgrading is possible after your current billing cycle ends. Cancel your current subscription, wait for it to expire, then subscribe to a lower plan. Alternatively, you can message to support and we'll make the downgrade for you!

### How do I add team members?

Team members are available on **Business plan and above**. Go to **main menu at the top-left \> Settings & Members \> Billing Plans \> Change Plan** and click **\+** to add members.

On the Professional plan, you can share individual profiles with users on the free plan as a workaround.

### What are the cloud browser concurrent session limits?

- **Professional:** 1 session
- **Business:** 2 sessions
- **Enterprise:** 3 sessions

These limits apply to cloud (Run in Cloud / web app) launches only. Local desktop launches are not limited by plan.

### Which cryptocurrency networks are supported?

GoLogin accepts **BTC, LTC, BCH, DOGE, and ETH (ERC-20 network only)**.

<Warning>
  If you send crypto on the wrong network (e.g., TRC-20, BEP-20), the payment **will not arrive and cannot be recovered**. Always verify the network before sending.
</Warning>

### My payment went through but my subscription wasn't updated.

Bank transfers may take several days to process. Try restarting the Gologin app. If the issue persists after 24 hours, contact support with your payment confirmation.

### I've got 2 GBs of residential traffic with my annual subscription. Does it get updated monthly or yearly?

Your 2 GBs of traffic get updated monthly on any annual subscription. If you've spent some of it during the month, it will get replenished the next month up to 2 GBs.

### Can I buy a Gologin subscription for less than 100 profiles?

You can purchase any of the current Professional plans that offer less than 100 profiles: Professional 10 profiles or Professional 50 profiles.

We currently don't offer setting a custom profile amount for plans with the profile count less than the Custom plan.

If you've registered before 2026 and don't have such subscription options, please contact human support so that the team can transfer your account to the new pricing.
