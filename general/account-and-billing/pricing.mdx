Gologin offers four paid tiers - Professional, Business, Enterprise, and Custom. All paid plans include 2GB of residential proxies and 24/7 support. Paying annually gives a **50% discount** compared to monthly billing.

You can always see the current pricing at [https://gologin.com/pricing/](https://gologin.com/pricing/) or in your workspace **Settings & Members** \> **Billing plans** \> **Change plan**

<Tip>
  These prices apply to accounts registered from **January 2026**. If you have an older account and want to switch to the new pricing, contact support.
</Tip>

## Plan descriptions

### Professional — from \$24/mo

For solo users. Available in three profile-count tiers (10, 50, or 100 profiles). Includes 1 simultaneous cloud launch and API access at 300 requests per minute.

### Business — from \$49/mo ⭐ Most Popular

For growing teams and agencies. Comes with 300 or 500 profiles, 1 team member slot, 100 profile shares, and 2 simultaneous cloud launches. **Team Members is a Business-and-above feature** — it is not available on Professional or Free.

### Enterprise — from \$99/mo

For scaling operations. 1,000 profiles, 3 simultaneous cloud launches, 1,000 profile shares, and 800 RPM API access.

### Custom — from \$149/mo

For large teams. Starts at 2,000 profiles and scales up to 100,000. Includes 4 simultaneous cloud launches and 1,200 RPM API access. Contact sales for tailored options.

### Forever Free

Three browser profiles, no time limit. Good for testing Gologin or very light personal use. See the [Free Plan Limitations](https://gologin.com/docs/#free-plan-limitations) section below for what's not included.

## Free Plan Limitations

The Forever Free plan includes 3 profiles and core Gologin features, but the following are **not available**:

- **Sharing profiles** with other users
- **Exporting profile cookies**
- **Bulk profile creation**
- **Adding team members**
- **Running profiles in the cloud**
- **Android Cloud and Proxy Devices**
- **Restoring deleted profiles**
- **API access**

## Annual vs. Monthly Billing

Annual plans are billed once per year and cost **50% less** than the equivalent monthly rate. For example, Professional is \$49/mo on monthly billing and \$24/mo on annual billing.

## FAQ

**Can I add more team members beyond the 1 included? What about the proxy traffic?**\
Yes - additional team member slots and can be purchased on Business, Enterprise, and Custom plans.\
Additional traffic purchase is available on any plan as well.

**What are residential proxies used for?**\
Each paid plan includes 2GB of residential proxy traffic built in, which you can use directly from Gologin without a third-party proxy provider.

**I registered before January 2026 — do these prices apply to me?**\
Not automatically. Your account remains on the legacy pricing. To migrate to the new plans, reach out to Gologin support.

**Is there a free trial?**\
New accounts get access to a 7-day free trial with full features before choosing a plan.
