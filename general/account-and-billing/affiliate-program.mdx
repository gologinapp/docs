title: "Affiliate program"
---

Gologin Affiliate Program lets you earn **10% of every payment** made by users you refer. There's no cap - every time a referred user renews or upgrades their subscription, you get a cut.

## How to get there

1. Click your **workspace name** in the top-left corner.
2. Select **Settings & Members**
3. In the left sidebar, click **Affiliate program**

<Frame>
  ![Affiliate Program](/images/affiliate_program.png)
</Frame>

Dashboard explained:

| Metric | Description |
| :-- | :-- |
| **Withdrawn** | Total earnings already paid out to you. |
| **Earned** | Total commissions earned since you joined the program. |
| **Available** | Balance ready to withdraw right now. |
| **Affiliate %** | Your current commission rate (default: 10%). |

## Creating Your referral ink

Enter a custom prefix in the **Store subdomain** field and click **Create referral link**. Your link will follow the format: `https://gologin.com/join/your-prefix`

Share this link anywhere - social media, blogs, YouTube descriptions, or directly with potential users.

## Tracking performance

The table below the link builder tracks all your referral activity:

- **Link** - which referral link was used
- **Clicks** - how many times the link was clicked
- **Registrations** - how many clicks converted to signups
- **Buyers** - how many signups converted to paying customers
- **Sum amount** - total revenue generated from that link

## Withdrawing earnings

Click **Withdrawal** to request a payout of your available balance. You can also review past payouts under **Payment history**.

<Info>
  We may request your payment address and other details to provide the payment. Please reach out to the support team if your payment isn't credited for more than one week.
</Info>
