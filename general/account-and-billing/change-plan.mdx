---
title: "Change and upgrade subscription"
---

You may want to change your subscription in case:

- You want to upgrade your current subscription tier
- You need to add a [Team members](/general/team-collaboration/team-members) feature to your plan or add more team member slots
- You have an idea of how much Gologin proxy traffic you spend every month and want it to be added automatically

## Upgrading subscription plan tier

<Note>
  You can only upgrade from one annual subscription to another annual, not monthly.
</Note>

**How it works.** When you upgrade the tier (for example, from Business to Enterprise):

- The cost of your current plan and the days spent are taken into account as **Proration discount**
- Your new renewal date becomes the date of the upgrade

To upgrade your current plan, follow this guide:

1. **Open the pricing page**
   - On the main All profiles page, just **click the cog icon** next to the plan info \> click **Change plan**.
     <Frame>
       ![Change Plan](/images/change_plan-1.png)
     </Frame>
2. **Choose the new plan**
   - Specify the team member and proxy traffic numbers and click **Upgrade plan** on your chosen subscription option.
   - Pick the **payment method** and proceed to checkout by clicking **Pay now**.
     <Frame>
       ![Upgrade Plan](/images/upgrade_plan.gif)
     </Frame>
3. **Complete checkout and start using Gologin**\
   After the checkout process is completed, your subscription plan will get upgraded shortly.

## Adding team members and proxy traffic to your current plan

**How it works:**

- the spent days of your subscription is taken into the account as **Proration discount**
- Your renewal date is unchanged

To subscribe to more add-ons on your current plan:

1. **Open the pricing page**
   - On the main All profiles page, just **click the cog icon** next to the plan info \> click **Change plan**.
     <Frame>
       ![Change Plan](/images/change_plan-2.png)
     </Frame>
2. **Specify the new add-ons**
   - Verify your current plan, it should have the **Renew the current plan** button.
   - Start adding more team member slots or add the proxy traffic.
   - Pick the **payment method** and proceed to checkout by clicking **Pay now**.
     <Frame>
       ![Add More Members And Traffic](/images/add_more_members_and_traffic.gif)
     </Frame>
3. **Complete checkout and start using Gologin**\
   After the checkout process is completed, your subscription plan will get updated shortly.
