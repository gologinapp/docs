## Changing your payment method

Currently, you cannot change the payment method without making a payment. Here's what you can do:

### Change payment method by purchasing a plan

1. Go to the **main menu at the top-left \>** **Settings & Members \> Billing Plans \> Payment Methods \> Change payment method.**
   <Frame>
     ![Change Payment Method](/images/change_payment_method.png)
   </Frame>
2. Renew the current plan or upgrade it. The payment method will get updated.
   1. your current plan will be counted in during the [plan upgrade](/general/account-and-billing/change-plan)

### Change payment method by removing the current one

1. Remove the current payment method in the **main menu at the top-left \>** **Settings & Members \> Billing Plans \> Payment Methods \> Cancel subscription.**
   <Frame>
     ![Cancel Subscription](/images/cancel_subscription.png)
   </Frame>
2. Wait for the current plan to elapse and purchase a new one with the new card or PayPal.

## Cancelling the subscription

If you want to cancel the current subscription, you just need to remove your payment method from your account. Go to the **main menu at the top-right \>** **Settings & Members \> Billing Plans \> Payment Methods \> Cancel subscription.**

## Additional payment methods info

### Cryptocurrency payments

Gologin accepts cryptocurrency payments in the following coins:

- **BTC** (Bitcoin)
- **LTC** (Litecoin)
- **BCH** (Bitcoin Cash)
- **DOGE** (Dogecoin)
- **ETH** (Ethereum — ERC-20 network only)

<Warning>
  Only send ETH on the **ERC-20 network**. If you send crypto on the wrong network (e.g., TRC-20, BEP-20), the payment **will not arrive and cannot be recovered**. Always double-check the network before confirming your transaction.
</Warning>

After sending a crypto payment, allow some time for the transaction to be confirmed on the blockchain. If your subscription is not updated after the transaction has been confirmed, restart the GoLogin app and wait up to 24 hours. If the issue persists, contact support with your transaction hash.

### US Bank account payments

Gologin accepts US bank account payments via Stripe. This method works differently from cards - the payment is not instant.

After completing your payment, expect a **delay of 3–5 business days** before it is credited to your account. Your Gologin subscription or plan will not activate until the funds are confirmed.

Keep this in mind if you're on a deadline - for immediate access, use a card or another instant payment method instead.

### Corporate card issues

Some corporate or business credit cards may be declined by our payment processor. If your corporate card is not accepted:

- Try using a **personal credit or debit card** instead.
- Use **PayPal** as an alternative payment method.
- Pay with **cryptocurrency** (see above).

If you need an invoice for your organization, go to **main menu at the top-right \> Settings & Members \> Billing Plans \> Payment History \> Download receipt**.
