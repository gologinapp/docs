---
title: "How to subscribe to Gologin"
---

You've tried Gologin out, liked it, and want to keep working?

Here's a simple guide on how to purchase a Gologin subscription!

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the pricing page">
    Click **Upgrade plan** or **Pay now** from All profiles menu.

    <Frame>
      ![Go To Pricing Page](/images/go_to_pricing_page.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose the subscription plan">
    You can pay **monthly** or choose the **annual subscription** (**which is 50% off!**)\
    Don't forget to set the [team members](/general/team-collaboration/team-members) and [proxy traffic](/api-reference/introduction/gologin-proxies)! In case you need more, you can always [upgrade your plan](/general/account-and-billing/change-plan) later.

    <Frame>
      ![Choose The Subscription](/images/choose_the_subscription.gif)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the payment method">
    Verify the plan options, payment amount, and **select the payment method**.\
    You can pay with **PayPal**, **credit or debit card**, or use **crypto payments**.

    <Frame>
      ![Choose Payment Method](/images/choose_payment_method.gif)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete checkout">
    Provide your payment details and **proceed with payment**.

    <Frame>
      ![Complete Checkout](/images/complete_checkout.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to your profiles and start using Gologin!">
    <Frame>
      ![Go To Profiles](/images/go_to_profiles.gif)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>
