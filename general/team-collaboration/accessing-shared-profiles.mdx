---
title: "Accessing shared profiles"
---

All the shared profiles will appear in your default workspace.

## Navigating the shared profile

To access them, just login into your Gologin account. All the profiles that has been shared to you are available on the **All profiles** page, marked with the **human icon** next to the profile name.

You can examine your **permissions** by clicking **3 dots on the profile \> Share**

<Frame>
  ![Accessing Shared Profile](/images/accessing_shared_profile.gif)
</Frame>

You can also **leave** the shared profile if you're done working with it by clicking **3 dots on the profile \> Leave.**
