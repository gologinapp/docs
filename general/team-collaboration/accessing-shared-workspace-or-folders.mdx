---
title: "Accessing shared workspace "
---

If you have been invited to a **workspace** or **shared folder**, you can access it after accepting the invitation sent to your email.

## If you are not registered yet

1. Open your **email inbox** and find the invitation message from **Gologin**.
2. Click the **invitation link** in the email.
3. Create your **Gologin account** by following the registration steps.

After registration, you will automatically gain access to the shared workspace.

## If you already have a Gologin account

1. Log in to your **Gologin account** using your credentials or **Google authentication**.
2. Open the **main menu** in the application.
3. Locate the shared workspace listed under your **account email**.
4. Click the workspace to open it.

Once selected, you will be able to access the profiles and folders shared with you.
