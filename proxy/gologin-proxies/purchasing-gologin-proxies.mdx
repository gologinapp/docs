---
title: "Purchasing Gologin Proxies"
---

Every paid Gologin plan comes with built-in proxy traffic. If you need more, there are two ways to get it - a **one-time top-up** or a **recurring add-on** tied to your subscription. This article explains both.

## Default proxy traffic

All paid plans include **2 GB of residential proxy traffic** per month, regardless of the plan tier or billing period (monthly or annual).

This traffic refreshes at the start of each billing month. It does not carry over - any unused traffic from the previous month is replaced with a fresh 2 GB.

<Check>
  Annual subscribers also receive 2 GB per month, not 2 GB per year. The refresh happens every month for the entire duration of the subscription.
</Check>

## Buying additional traffic

### One-time top-up

A top-up is a one-time purchase of extra traffic. Use this if you have an occasional spike in usage and do not need more traffic every month.

**Steps:**

1. On the main **All profiles** page, click the traffic amount next to the **Proxies** tab in the left side-menu.
2. Select the **proxy type** you want to buy traffic for.
3. Choose the **amount of traffic** you need.
4. Click the **\+** button next to your chosen amount and proceed to checkout.

</Frame>

<Info>
  Prices shown in the app are the most up to date. Check them directly before purchasing.
</Info>

**Payment methods for top-ups:**

You can pay with a credit or debit card, or with crypto. Users from some countries - such as Russia and Belarus - may not be able to pay by card for top-ups. If your card payment is declined, use crypto instead.

### Subscription add-on

A subscription add-on adds a set amount of proxy traffic to your plan on a recurring basis. This means the traffic is added automatically every month.

You can add traffic to your subscription during initial checkout or at any time by changing your current plan.

**How to add traffic to your current plan:**

1. On the main **All profiles** page, click the **cog icon** next to your plan info at the bottom-left.
2. Click **Change plan**.
3. On the pricing page, confirm your plan - you should see the **Renew the current plan** button, which means you are adding to your current plan rather than changing it.
4. Find the **proxy traffic** add-on and set the amount you want added each month.
5. Click **Upgrade now**, choose the payment method and click **Pay now** to complete checkout.

<Frame>
  ![Proxy With Subscription](/images/proxy_with_subscription.gif)
</Frame>

**How recurring traffic works:**

- **Monthly plan** - the add-on amount is charged and added every month when your subscription renews.
- **Annual plan** - the add-on is paid once as part of your annual subscription and added to your account every month throughout the year.

For more details on changing your plan, see [Change and upgrade subscription](/general/account-and-billing/change-plan).

## Viewing available Gologin proxy traffic

To view how much traffic is left, just hover over the current available traffic in the left menu. You will see the breakdown of how much traffic is left for each of the proxy types.
