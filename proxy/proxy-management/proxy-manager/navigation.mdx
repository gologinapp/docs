title: "Navigation"
---

All the proxy navigation is available right from the **All profiles** page. Just click on the **Proxy & Location field** of the profile and start editing.

### Adding a proxy

To add a single or multiple proxies:

1. Copy your proxy
2. Hover over the **Proxy & Location** column on your profile
3. Click the **Paste proxies** button
   - you can also use the system **hot keys** to paste the proxies

<Frame>
  ![Adding Proxy](/images/adding_proxy.gif)
</Frame>

### Checking a proxy

To check if the proxy is working: just **click the arrows** next to the proxy name on the **All Profiles** page.\
It will show the proxy country and the current outgoing IP.

<Frame>
  ![Checking Proxy](/images/checking_proxy.gif)
</Frame>

### Editing proxy settings

To edit the proxy settings such as IP or password: **click on the proxy** \> **click the pencil icon** \> click **Save** after making changes.

<Frame>
  ![Editing Proxy](/images/editing_proxy.gif)
</Frame>

### Unlinking proxy from profile

To remove a proxy from the profile without deleting it: **click on the proxy**, then **click the X to unlink** it.

<Frame>
  ![Unlink Proxy](/images/unlink_proxy.gif)
</Frame>

### Deleting a proxy

To delete a proxy, **click on any proxy** to open the proxy manager \> **right-click** on the proxy you want to delete \> click **Delete proxy** \> **confirm** your choice.

<Frame>
  ![Deleting Proxy](/images/deleting_proxy.gif)
</Frame>

### Copying proxies

To copy a proxy, **click on any proxy** to open the proxy manager \> **right-click** on the proxy you want to copy \> click **Copy proxy**.

<Frame>
  ![Copy Proxy](/images/copy_proxy.gif)
</Frame>

### Bulk proxy actions

You can copy or delete **multiple proxies at a time**. Just click anywhere on the **Proxy & Location** column to enter the proxy manager \> **right-click on the proxy** \> **Select proxy** \> keep selecting the proxies you want to act on \> click **Copy** or **Delete** at the bottom of the Proxy Manager window.

<Frame>
  ![Bulk Proxy Actions](/images/bulk_proxy_actions.gif)
</Frame>
