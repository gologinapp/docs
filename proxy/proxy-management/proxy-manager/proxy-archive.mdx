---
title: "Proxy Archive"
---

The **Proxy Archive** is where Gologin automatically moves proxies that are no longer in use. To open it, go to **Proxies** in the left sidebar, then click the **Archive** tab.

A proxy is moved to the archive after it has not been used for **30 days**. Archived proxies are not visible in the Proxy Manager or on the main Proxies page - they won't clutter your active proxy list.

If a proxy stays in the archive without being used, it is permanently deleted after another **180 days**.

## Restoring proxies from proxy archive

To restore a proxy, select it using the checkbox and click **Restore**. You can select multiple proxies and restore them all at once.
