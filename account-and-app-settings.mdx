To reach the Gologin app **main menu**, click on the **burger-menu at the top left**.

Key elements:

- Info on your current account and subscription
- the **Change plan** button
- **Buy proxy** - for purchasing Gologin proxies
- **New profile** - creates a new Android profile
- **Folders manager** - creating, deleting or rearranging your folders
- **Workspace list** - here you will see the names of your current workspaces
- **Add workspace** - to add a new one
- **Support** - reach out if you have any questions or issues!
- **Settings** - other Gologin account settings
- **Log out** - logs you out of the account

To go to the **main settings** of your account, just click **Settings in the main menu**.

Elements:

- **Overview** - to change your email or the current plan
- **Billing plans** - informs you about the payment history, lets you input the promo code and purchase a new plan
- **Add members** - for adding new team members to your workspace (Business plan tier and higher).
- **Two-step verification** - set up 2FA to enter by one-time codes
- **Active sessions** - to check and terminate other active sessions for your account
- **Useful links** - links to our YouTube channel, blogs and support
- **Restore profiles** - for restoring deleted profiles
