Guides for building and managing accounts that maintain long-term access across platforms.

Each guide in this section focuses on a specific platform and covers the behavioral patterns that matter most — from initial setup through day-to-day activity. The goal is to help you understand how platforms evaluate accounts, so you can make informed decisions rather than follow arbitrary rules.
