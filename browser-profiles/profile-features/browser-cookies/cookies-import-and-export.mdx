title: "Cookies import and export"
---

To see if you have any cookies in the profile, just click **3 dots** on it \> **Cookies**.

Here you can **import**, **export** or **delete** cookies.

<Frame>
  ![Cookies Menu](/images/cookies_menu.gif)
</Frame>

## How to import cookies into Gologin profile

Gologin supports **JSON**, **Netscape** and **plain text** formats. It will accept cookies exported from **Gologin directly** and cookie files created by **third-party Chrome extensions**, such as: EditThisCookie, Swap My Cookies, Cookie Editor, etc.

<Note>
  If you have any issues during import, verify if the supplied cookies are in the supported format.
</Note>

To make the import:

1. Click **3 dots on the profile** \> **Cookies**
2. Hit **Import cookies**
3. Click the **file image** or **drag your cookie file** to it\
   you can also copy-paste cookies as a plain text using the field below it
4. Click **Import**

<Frame>
  ![Cookies Import](/images/cookies_import.gif)
</Frame>

Your profile is ready for the launch 👍

## Cookie export

To export cookies using the built-in menu:

1. Click **3 dots on the profile** \> **Cookies**
2. Hit **Export**
3. Choose the **folder** and click **Save**
