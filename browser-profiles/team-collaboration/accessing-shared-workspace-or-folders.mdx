---
title: "Accessing shared Workspace or Folders"
---

You've been added to a workspace or a folder and don't know how to access it?

## If you are not registered yet

1. After you've been invited, **access the email** inbox and **find the invite message** from GoLogin
2. Use the **link** in the email to create your GoLogin account

## After you've registered

1. **Login into the account** using your credentials or Google Authentication
2. Navigate to the **main menu** and locate the shared workspace under your email
3. Click on the **shared workspace**
