---
title: "Restore deleted profiles"
---

Gologin retains your profiles for at least **120 hours** after deletion, allowing you to recover accidentally deleted profiles from the **Restore profiles** menu. The feature is available on any paid plan!

## How to restore your profiles

1. Open the **dropdown menu in the top-right corner**
2. Navigate to **Settings & Members**
3. Go to the **Restore profiles** tab
4. Look for the profiles that needs recovering and click **Restore**

![GoLogin_restore-profiles.gif](/images/GoLogin_restore-profiles.gif)

<Note>Profiles deleted more than 120 hours ago cannot be restored.
If you can't find the lost profiles, try looking for them by the profile name using the search feature or contact support for assistance.</Note>
