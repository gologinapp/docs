title: "FAQ: Android"
---

### Is the Android app completely free?

The app is free to download and use with your existing Gologin plan. Features depend on your subscription (profile limits apply).

### Can I run multiple profiles simultaneously on Android?

No — for stability, the app runs one profile at a time. Desktop version supports parallel runs.

### Does it work with emulators?

The app is designed for real physical Android devices. Using it on emulators is not recommended and may reduce fingerprint quality.

### How do profiles sync between desktop and Android?

Log in with the same account — changes sync across devices.

### Where can I get support?

Contact support via the app, email [**support@gologin.com**](mailto:support@gologin.com), or the official website.
