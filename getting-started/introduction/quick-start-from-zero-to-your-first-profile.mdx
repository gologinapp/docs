## Step-by-step

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download the Gologin app">
    👉 [Download the Gologin app for any supported system.](https://gologin.com/download/)\
    Check [Supported platforms & installation](/getting-started/setup/supported-platforms-installation) for details. <img src="/images/Gologin_download_page.png" alt="Gologin_download_page.png" />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Register in Gologin using your **email or continue with Google** <img src="/images/GoLogin_registration.png" alt="GoLogin_registration.png" />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a profile">
    Create a profile or couple of profiles for your accounts.\
    Your profiles will use **the most suiting settings and get unique fingerprints by default**. <img src="/images/GoLogin_quick_profile.png" alt="GoLogin_quick_profile.png" />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a proxy">
    **Changing your IP is a crucial step in hiding your identity.**\
    Choose a built-in proxy or add your own.\
    Your geolocation will match your new IP location automatically. <img src="/images/GoLogin_change_IP.gif" alt="GoLogin_change_IP.gif" />
  </Step>
  <Step title="You're ready to use your profile!">
    Hit **Run** to launch your profile and browse with a fresh digital identity 🚀 <img src="/images/GoLogin_run_profile.gif" alt="GoLogin_run_profile.gif" />
  </Step>
</Steps>
