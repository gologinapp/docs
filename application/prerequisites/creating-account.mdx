## Getting Started with GoLogin

Before you can start using GoLogin's anti-detect browser capabilities, you'll need to create and set up your account. This guide walks you through the entire process from registration to your first login.

## Creating a New Account

### Step 1: Visit the Registration Page

1. Navigate to the [GoLogin official website](https://gologin.com)
2. Click on the **Sign Up** button in the top-right corner of the homepage

### Step 2: Choose Your Registration Method

GoLogin offers several ways to create your account:

- Register with your email address
- Sign up with Google
- Sign up with Facebook

For the most control over your account, we recommend using the email registration option.

### Step 3: Complete the Registration Form

If registering with email:

1. Enter your email address
2. Create a secure password (minimum 8 characters with mixed case, numbers, and special characters)
3. Review and accept the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy
4. Click **Create Account**

### Step 4: Verify Your Email

1. Check your email inbox for a verification message from GoLogin
2. Click the verification link in the email
3. If you don't see the email, check your spam folder or request a new verification email

## Choosing Your Subscription Plan

After verifying your email, you'll need to select a subscription plan:

1. **Free Plan**: Limited functionality with basic features
2. **Standard Plan**: Expanded capabilities for individual users
3. **Professional Plan**: Full functionality for power users
4. **Team Plan**: Enterprise features for team collaboration

Each plan offers different limits on the number of browser profiles, team members, and available features.

## Setting Up Your Account

### Completing Your Profile

For the best experience, complete your account profile:

1. Navigate to **Account Settings**
2. Add your full name and any other requested information
3. Set your preferred timezone and language
4. Add a profile picture (optional but recommended for team accounts)

### Setting Up Two-Factor Authentication (Recommended)

Enhance your account security by enabling two-factor authentication:

1. Go to **Security Settings**
2. Select **Enable Two-Factor Authentication**
3. Choose your preferred 2FA method (authenticator app or SMS)
4. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the setup

## Downloading the GoLogin Application

After creating your account, you'll need to download and install the GoLogin application:

1. Navigate to the **Downloads** section of your dashboard
2. Select the appropriate version for your operating system (Windows, macOS, or Linux)
3. Follow the installation instructions for your platform
4. Launch the application after installation

## Logging In For the First Time

1. Open the GoLogin application
2. Enter your email and password
3. If you've enabled 2FA, enter your authentication code
4. Accept any permissions requested by the application
